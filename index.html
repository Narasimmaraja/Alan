Recipe

   To start, cook one cup of white or brown rice by rinsing it thoroughly and then simmering it in two cups of water until it's tender and the water is absorbed. While the rice is cooking, dice boneless chicken (such as chicken breast or thighs) into bite-sized pieces. In a separate pan, heat a tablespoon of olive or vegetable oil and cook the chicken until it's fully cooked and no longer pink. Optionally, you can include vegetables like carrots, peas, or green beans, which should be steamed or boiled until tender and then chopped. Combine the cooked chicken, rice, and optional vegetables in a large bowl, mixing everything together. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature before serving it to your dog
